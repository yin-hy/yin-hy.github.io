About

I am advised by Prof. Bruno Sinopoli and Prof. Xudong Chen in the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis.

I received my B.E. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from ShanghaiTech University in 2020, proudly among its third cohort of bachelor students since the university was founded in 2013. At ShanghaiTech, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Boris Houska and Prof. Youlong Wu.

I also spent seven wonderful months at The Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a member of Prof. Jun Zhang's group in 2021.
