About me

I received my B.E. degree in Electronic Information Engineering from ShanghaiTech University in 2020.
During my stay at ShanghaiTech, I was fortunate to work with Prof. Boris Houska and Prof. Youlong Wu.

After that, I was very grateful to spend seven wonderful months at Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a member of Prof. Jun Zhang's group, where I met great friends and professors.

Currently, I am a third-year PhD Candidate under the supervision of Prof. Bruno Sinopoli in the Department of Electrical and Systems Engineering, Washington University in St. Louis.
